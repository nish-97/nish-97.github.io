About Me

Hello, I am a 3rd year MS student in CSE Department at IIT Bombay. I have completed my B.Tech in Information Technology from Techno India, Kolkata. I love learning about systems/components present at the lowest layer of abstraction in the computing stack, from designing optimal compiler optimisations to pushing the boundaries of processor performance. My research interests include topics related to Computer Architecture, Compilers and Operating Systems. My current work is focused on securing against Transient execution based microarchitectural attacks for end-to-end memory hierarchy; guided by Prof. Biswabandan Panda as a part of the CASPER research group.

Besides academics, I love following world geopolitics, cricket and chess. I also firmly believe in sustainable development of the planet Earth through education and awareness, so that we can curb the effects of climate change.

“I’m sure the moment we start behaving as if we were in an emergency, we can avoid climate and ecological catastrophe.” – Greta Thunberg
