Hello, I am a 1st year MS student in CSE Department at IIT Bombay. I come from Patna, Bihar and have completed my Undergraduate in Information Technology from Techno India, Kolkata. I love learning about systems/components present at the lowest layer of abstraction of the compute stack, right from Compilers to the Processors. My research interests include topics related to Computer Architecture and Operating Systems. My current work is focused on evaluating various Microarchitectural Security threats and its mitigation, under the supervision of Prof. Biswabandan Panda as a part of CASPER Research group.

Besides academics, I love following world geopolitics, cricket and chess. I also firmly believe in sustainable development of the planet Earth through education and awareness, so that we can curb the effects of climate change.

“I’m sure the moment we start behaving as if we were in an emergency, we can avoid climate and ecological catastrophe.” - Greta Thunberg
